# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Tamil cricket club players based in Plymouth, Devon. Mostly mobile users — they check the app quickly to see upcoming matches, confirm availability, and find out if they've been selected. The captain and team manager use the admin panel to manage matches, select the XI, and send messages to players. The community is tight-knit and proud of their Tamil heritage and cricket identity.

## Product Purpose

Tamil United CC's internal team management app. Players submit availability for upcoming matches; the captain selects the XI and publishes it; the manager sends messages to the squad. Secondary surfaces include a live league table and player statistics. Success means every player responds before the deadline and the captain can confirm the team without chasing anyone on WhatsApp.

## Brand Personality

Proud, community-rooted, competitive. Not flashy for the sake of it — this club has history (formerly DTU CC, Est. 1977) and the design should carry that weight. Warm, not corporate. Feels like it belongs to the players, not like a generic SaaS product.

## Anti-references

- Too minimal: no sparse white-space-heavy apps that feel cold or blank
- Generic sports SaaS (ESPN-style blue grids, over-corporate dashboards)
- Consumer-app playfulness that undermines the competitive cricket identity
- Anything that looks like it was assembled from a template

## Design Principles

1. **Carry the badge.** The DTU shield and green-gold palette are identity, not decoration. Let them lead.
2. **Mobile first, always.** Players check the app between overs on their phone. Every screen must work perfectly at 390px.
3. **Clarity under time pressure.** A player needs to know their availability status and match details in under 3 seconds. No hunting.
4. **Proud, not loud.** Rich and confident without being garish. Depth over decoration.
5. **The captain's tool.** Admin surfaces should feel authoritative and efficient, not identical to the player-facing screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG AA. No specific known needs raised. Ensure adequate contrast for outdoor mobile use (bright sunlight on a cricket pitch).
